FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Everything you need to know before register.

What's your refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the conference. After that, tickets can be transfered to another attendee.

Is there a virtual attendance option?

Yes. Every keynote and selected workshops are streamed online with access to session recordings.Please contact the official email id of DevConf2026.

Do you offer group or team discounts?

Yes. Team packages are available for up to 10 attendees with exclusive benefits and discounted pricing.

What's included in each ticket tier?

Each plan includes conference access, while Pro and Team tickets include additional workshops, networking events and exclusive benefits.

Are talks recorded for later viewing?

Absolutely. Every attendee receives access to recorded sessions depending on their ticket package.

What's your code of conduct?

DevConf is committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free environment for everyone regardless of background or experience.
